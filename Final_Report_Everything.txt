2) ROUTING ENGINE (TMS_Ledger.py)
- The app intentionally bypasses Streamlit's native `pages/` auto-router.
- `TMS_Ledger.py` declares a `TAB_ROUTES` dictionary mapping 8 sidebar labels to concrete Python files in `Tabs/*`.
- A custom `run_tab(path, storage)` loader uses `importlib.util.spec_from_file_location(...)` + `exec_module(...)`.
- Contract: every tab module must expose `render(storage)`; otherwise the router raises an in-app error.
- A single `DataStorage` instance is initialized once in `TMS_Ledger.py` and passed to every tab, so all tabs share one consistent data layer.
- Sidebar uses `st.radio` to select one of the 8 tabs and dispatches through `run_tab(...)`.
- Authentication gate is handled by `check_login()` before routing.

5) MARKET PREDICTOR LOGIC SUMMARY (Services/app/market_predictor.py)
A) VPVR + POC
- Close prices are binned into 20 horizontal bins.
- Volume is summed per price bin to build a volume profile.
- Point of Control (POC) is the price bin with maximum summed volume.
- Signals:
  - BUY: current close within В±2% of POC and current close > previous close.
  - SELL: current close is more than 3% below POC.

B) Smart Money Concepts: Fair Value Gaps (FVG)
- Bullish FVG if Low(candle3) > High(candle1).
- Bearish FVG if High(candle3) < Low(candle1).
- Rectangles are rendered on chart for detected zones.
- Signals:
  - BUY: current candle closes green while price enters a bullish FVG.
  - SELL/DUMP: current candle closes red while price enters a bearish FVG.

C) Wyckoff Accumulation Scanner
- Computes 20-day rolling std dev of Close (volatility).
- Computes 20-day and 200-day SMA of Close.
- STRONG BUY (Accumulation) when:
  - 20-day volatility is in bottom 10th percentile of historical volatility, and
  - price is within 5% of 200-day SMA.
- Alert text: "Wyckoff Spring Alert: Accumulation detected. Watch for breakout."
